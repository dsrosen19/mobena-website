Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Mobena.

What is an ALA?
ALA stands for Asset, Liability, or Activity — the building blocks of your financial model in Mobena. Every investment, trust, entity, loan, and income source is entered as an ALA. The number of ALAs in your plan determines which pricing tier is right for you.
How is Mobena different from other tax planning software?
Most tax planning software was built for a different era — single-year projections, no support for complex assets, and no integration between income tax, estate planning, and portfolio. Mobena is the first platform to unify all three, calculate actual tax (Form 1040 and 1041), model any family structure including grantor trusts, tiered partnerships, and alternative assets, and project up to 50+ years forward.
Who is Mobena designed for?
Mobena is designed for ultra high net worth families and the advisors who serve them — CPAs, estate attorneys, financial advisors, family offices, and RIAs. Individual families can use Mobena independently, and professional firms can use it to serve their clients with a shared, unified model.
What estate planning strategies does Mobena model?
Mobena models every major wealth transfer strategy used by UHNW families: GRATs, SLATs, IDGTs, CLATs, CLUTs, QPRTs, ILITs, CRTs, FLPs, BDITs, DAFs, QSBS §1202, and more. Strategies can be combined and layered — and every technique is modeled with its correct income tax consequences, not just the transfer tax effect.
How accurate is Mobena's tax calculation?
Mobena calculates actual tax — running Form 1040 and Form 1041 calculations, not approximations or estimates. Grantor trust income is correctly reported on the grantor's return. Non-grantor trust taxation uses correct compressed trust rates with proper DNI treatment. State tax is calculated accurately across all 50 states.
Does Mobena offer a free trial?
Yes — individual and family plans include a 3-month free trial with no credit card required. Commercial clients (CPAs, law firms, and family offices) receive a dedicated trial period as part of the onboarding process. Contact us to get started.
Can my entire advisory team use Mobena?
Yes. Mobena is designed for collaboration — the CPA, estate attorney, financial advisor, and the family can all work from the same live model. Commercial plans support multi-user teams. Contact us for pricing on team access.
