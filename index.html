Income Tax Planning

The entire family's income tax. One model. Calculated, not estimated.

Mobena runs actual income tax calculations — Form 1040, Form 1041, state returns — for the entire family unit: individuals, grantor trusts, non-grantor trusts, partnerships, S-corps, LLCs, and private entities. Not estimates. Actual tax, projected 5, 10, even 50 years forward.

Form 1040 and 1041 — calculated correctly, not approximated
Grantor trust income reported on grantor's return — modeled correctly (most software gets this wrong)
Non-grantor trust taxation at compressed trust rates with correct DNI and distributable income treatment
Tiered partnerships, S-corps, LLCs — all pass-through entities supported with correct K-1 treatment
Real estate: passive losses, carryforwards, depreciation, 1031 exchanges modeled over time
Complete state tax model — accurate multi-state calculations across all 50 states
Multi-year projections: 5, 10, even 50 years — see the full tax trajectory, not just this year
Estate Tax Planning

Every estate technique. Integrated income tax. Decades of precision.

Model any family's full balance sheet for estate tax — including complex and alternative assets — and layer on any combination of estate planning strategies. Mobena integrates estate tax and income tax together, the way a top CPA would.

Every major wealth transfer strategy: GRAT, SLAT, IDGT, CLAT, CLUT, QPRT, ILIT, CRT, FLP, BDIT, QSBS §1202, and more
Layer multiple strategies — combine a SLAT with an IDGT, stack GRATs, model a full comprehensive plan
Alternative assets supported: real estate, private equity, closely-held businesses, notes, royalties
Correct income tax at death — basis step-up, IRD assets, continued holding inside trusts all modeled accurately
Scenario comparison — see No Plan vs. GRAT vs. SLAT+IDGT side by side with full income tax consequences
50+ year multigenerational waterfall — project wealth transfer across generations, not just first death
